### What?

Future perfect digital are a visual technology studio specialising in web-based 3D content and mixed reality experiences. 
I created an interactive webgl based experience based off of their mockups. 
A part of the requirements was a mobile first approach so lots of time was spent making sure the 3D scenes were optimised to run on mobile devices.

### Tech?

- Three js
- React three fiber
- GSAP 3
- Tailwind css
